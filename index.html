How it works

From raw material to finished video, in software you run

01

Connect your source material

Add clips, calls, audio, ideas, or docs. Set your brand context once and it persists across every project.

02

AI builds your template

Your prompt becomes a reusable template. The AI generates hooks, scripts, captions, audio, and structure, filling in whatever is missing.

03

Review, rank & publish

Compare outputs, approve the best, and flag what to improve. Every ranking makes the next run more accurate.

Who it's for

Built for anyone who needs more content than they can consistently produce

Direct businesses

Businesses that need recurring on-brand video but don't have the budget or team to produce it consistently. Run the software, not production.

Agencies

More production capacity across many client workspaces without hiring more editors. Each client gets its own brand memory, roles, and approval flows.

Expert-led businesses

Coaches, founders, and educators with calls, webinars, and ideas that never become content. Turn existing knowledge into a consistent clip stream.
